About Me

I'm an Instructional Designer with a Ph.D. in Second Language Acquisition and Instructional Technology (University of South Florida, GPA 3.94). My career spans higher education and federal training environments, where I've designed and delivered learning solutions for thousands of adult learners.

Currently at the Defense Language Institute, I lead curriculum modernization using Canvas LMS, Articulate Storyline, and SCORM-based tools. I'm also actively exploring how generative AI can accelerate content creation without sacrificing quality or accuracy.

I believe great learning design removes barriers and gives people exactly what they need — when they need it.
